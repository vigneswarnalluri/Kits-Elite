History of ELITE


The ELITE Club was established in 2020 through the collaborative efforts of faculty members, enthusiastic students, and the support of the Head of the Department. The club was founded with the primary objective of fostering active student participation in various AI-related workshops, innovations, and cultural activities.


By providing a platform for intellectual and creative engagement, the club aims to cultivate a spirit of harmony and collaboration within the Department of Artificial Intelligence, ensuring holistic growth and a thriving academic community.


AI At A Glance


Our AI Department drives technological innovation through three specialized teams:

1. Machine Learning Engineering - Develops and deploys AI solutions
2. Data Science & Analytics - Creates predictive models and insights
3. Research & Development - Explores emerging AI technologies

We focus on natural language processing, computer vision, and deep learning while maintaining high standards for ethical AI development and data privacy. Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the capability of computational systems to perform tasks typically associated with human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and decision-making. It is a field of research in computer science that develops and studies methods and software that enable machines to perceive their environment and use learning and intelligence to take actions that maximize their chances of achieving defined goals.
